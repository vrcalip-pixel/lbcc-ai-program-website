Program overview

Learning AI from the ground up

This sample landing page presents a possible AI program designed for students who are new to the field. The program would combine foundational technology concepts with hands-on practice, responsible use guidelines, and clear links to academic and workforce goals.

Course pathways

Flexible options for different goals

AI Foundations

Build core vocabulary, understand common AI tools, and learn how data, models, and prompts work together.

Applied AI Skills

Practice using AI to support research, writing, analysis, productivity, and creative problem-solving.

Ethics and Careers

Study privacy, bias, accessibility, and professional expectations while exploring transfer and employment opportunities.

Student benefits

Support for academic and career growth

  • Beginner-friendly lessons that do not assume prior AI experience.
  • Hands-on projects connected to real academic and workplace tasks.
  • Discussion of responsible AI use, including fairness and transparency.
  • Preparation for further study, certificates, internships, or entry-level roles.

Contact and next steps

Start with curiosity

Students interested in this concept could begin by meeting with an academic counselor, reviewing technology course options, and identifying how AI skills connect to their major or career plans.
